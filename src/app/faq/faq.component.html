Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find a carpool match?

When you log in, a map will show up displaying available routes and drivers, allowing you to find a carpool match that suits your needs.

What should I do if I have a problem with my carpool?

If you're having issues, please contact us through the 'Support' section.

What is the radius of the app?

The app covers a radius of 100 kilometers from ATU Sligo.

Do drivers stop on the routes to college to pick people up?

Yes, drivers may make stops on their routes to pick up other passengers.
