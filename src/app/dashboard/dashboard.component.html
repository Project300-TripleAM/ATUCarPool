Driver Guidelines

Here are some guidelines for drivers to ensure a safe and efficient carpool experience:

  • Ensure your vehicle is in good condition and meets safety standards.
  • Always communicate with passengers regarding trip details and expectations.
  • Respect passengers' preferences and adhere to agreed-upon pickup and drop-off locations.
  • Be punctual and considerate of passengers' time.

Best Practices for Drivers

Follow these best practices to provide an exceptional carpooling service:

  • Keep your car clean and comfortable for passengers.
  • Be open to feedback and suggestions from passengers to improve your service.
  • Adhere to traffic laws and regulations at all times.
  • Ensure a pleasant and courteous attitude towards passengers.

Safety and Security Tips For Passengers

Here are some tips to ensure safety and security during trips:

  • Always verify the driver and vehicle details before boarding.
  • Share trip details with trusted contacts.
  • Choose well-lit and populated pickup/drop-off locations.
  • Trust your instincts; if something feels wrong, cancel the trip.

Best Practices for Using the App

Follow these best practices for a seamless experience:

  • Keep your app updated to the latest version.
  • Rate your trips to provide feedback and improve service.
  • Report any issues or concerns to customer support.
  • Show respect to fellow passengers & drivers.
