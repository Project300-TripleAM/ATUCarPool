Safety and Security Tips

Here are some tips to ensure safety and security during trips:

  • Always verify the driver and vehicle details before boarding.
  • Share trip details with trusted contacts.
  • Choose well-lit and populated pickup/drop-off locations.
  • Trust your instincts; if something feels wrong, cancel the trip.

Best Practices for Using the App

Follow these best practices for a seamless experience:

  • Keep your app updated to the latest version.
  • Rate your trips to provide feedback and improve service.
  • Report any issues or concerns to customer support.
  • Show respect to fellow users.
